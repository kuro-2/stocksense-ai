How it works

Hard data first.
AI judgement second.

We never let the model guess raw numbers. Live prices and indicators are computed from the market — the AI only does the reasoning on top.

01

Live market data

The moment you search, we pull a live quote and 90 days of price history — current price, % change, 52-week range, market cap, P/E.

02

Local technicals

RSI, SMA 20/50/200, support & resistance and trend are computed locally from real prices — never invented by the AI.

03

AI reasoning

Claude receives the price, indicators and sector as structured context, then produces a recommendation, target, stop loss, F&O idea and risks.

04

One clear page

It all merges into a single, scannable read — recommendation card, chart, technicals, F&O panel, news and risk warnings.
